BOM rules
Liquor BOM: 70% roasted chocolate (DRGS) + 30% fat
Fat type: CBS = non-tempering (East Mac) · CBE = tempering (West Mac)
EU/US: Cannot be mixed on the same batch on the mass line (Refining / Conching / Depositing). Does not apply to the Mcintyres.
Roasting yield: ~68–73%. First batch post-changeover may be scrap/QA hold.
